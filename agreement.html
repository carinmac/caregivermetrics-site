Before you begin.

This is a small, invitation-only beta. Please read the agreement below carefully before signing. It takes about three minutes. Your access to the app is provided immediately after you submit.

This Agreement is made between Carin Ann MacInroy (the "Owner") and you (the "Tester"), as of the date you sign below.

Background

Owner has developed Caregiver Metrics, a validated methodology for measuring and quantifying unpaid caregiver burden through structured event logging, cognitive load assessment, and emotional pattern tracking (the "System"). Owner wishes to have the System tested before commercial launch. Tester wishes to participate in this testing and, in exchange for free access to the System, agrees to provide feedback and keep the System confidential.

1. Access to the System

Owner grants Tester permission to access and use the System solely for testing purposes during the testing period, which runs from the date of this Agreement for six months, or until Owner ends the testing, whichever comes first. This permission is personal to Tester and cannot be shared or transferred. Owner may end Tester's access at any time by providing written notice.

2. What Tester Agrees to Do

Tester agrees to use the System following any instructions provided by Owner; provide honest feedback about the System, including observations about what works well and what could be improved; complete any assessments, questionnaires, or feedback forms provided by Owner; and promptly tell Owner about any problems or errors encountered.

3. Ownership

The System belongs entirely to Owner. This includes its methodology, structure, assessments, questionnaires, scoring methods, training materials, and all related content. Nothing in this Agreement gives Tester any ownership rights in the System.

Any feedback, suggestions, or ideas Tester provides about the System will belong to Owner. Tester assigns all rights in such feedback to Owner, and Owner may use it freely without any payment to Tester.

All data generated through Tester's use of the System, including completed assessments and results, belongs to Owner. Owner may use this data in anonymized form for research, validation, and marketing purposes.

4. Keeping Things Confidential

Tester agrees to keep confidential all information about the System, including its content, methodology, materials, and any business information shared by Owner. Tester will not share this information with anyone else or use it for any purpose other than participating in this beta test.

These confidentiality obligations continue for five years after this Agreement ends.

5. Testimonials

If Tester provides a testimonial or endorsement about the System, Owner may use it for marketing purposes with Tester's prior approval. Tester will not make public statements about the System or this beta test without Owner's written consent.

6. No Payment

Tester's only compensation for participating is the opportunity to use the System during the testing period. Owner will not pay Tester any fees or other amounts under this Agreement.

7. The System Is Provided As Is

The System is being tested and may contain errors or incomplete features. Owner makes no promises about whether the System will work perfectly or produce accurate results. The System is for informational purposes only and is not a substitute for professional advice. Tester uses the System at Tester's own risk.

Owner will not be responsible for any harm or damages arising from Tester's use of the System, except to the maximum extent prohibited by law.

8. Returning Materials

When this Agreement ends or if Owner requests, Tester will return or delete all copies of the System and any materials provided by Owner.

9. General Terms

This Agreement is the entire agreement between the parties on this subject. It can only be changed in writing signed by both parties. If any part is found unenforceable, the rest remains in effect. This Agreement is governed by the laws of Ontario and Canada. It may be signed in counterparts and by electronic signature.
